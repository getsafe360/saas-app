## Pricing Psychology

### Why $19?

1. **Price Anchoring:** Under $20 feels "inexpensive" for B2B SaaS
2. **Psychological Threshold:** $19 vs. $20 triggers "under $20" perception
3. **Competitive Advantage:** Significantly cheaper than $49-99 competitors
4. **High Volume Potential:** Lower price = larger addressable market
5. **Upgrade Path:** Easy upsell from free ($0) to affordable ($19)

### Why Free Tier?

1. **Acquisition:** No-risk trial, viral growth potential
2. **Product-Led Growth:** Users experience value before paying
3. **Conversion Funnel:** Clear upgrade path when users hit limits
4. **Market Education:** Build awareness in price-sensitive segments
5. **WordPress Community:** Free tier appeals to WordPress hobbyists

### Why "Custom" for Enterprise?

1. **Value-Based Pricing:** Price based on organizational value, not features
2. **Qualification:** Filters out non-serious inquiries
3. **Flexibility:** Negotiate based on volume, usage, integrations
4. **High-Touch Sales:** Builds relationships, understands needs
5. **Higher Margins:** Enterprise deals typically $5K-50K/year
